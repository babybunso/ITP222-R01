## Review / Critique Expectations

For **each paper**, your handwritten review should clearly address the following points:

1. **Problem Statement**
   - What problem does the paper aim to solve?
   - Why is this problem relevant to Systems Integration?

2. **Approach / Methodology**
   - What system, architecture, or integration approach is proposed?
   - What technologies, models, or frameworks are used?

3. **Key Contributions**
   - What are the main contributions of the paper?
   - What is new, useful, or significant about the work?

4. **Strengths and Limitations**
   - What does the paper do well?
   - What assumptions, limitations, or gaps do you observe?

5. **Capstone / Research Relevance**
   - How can this paper inspire a **Systems Integration research or capstone topic**?
   - What can be improved, extended, or integrated with other systems?

Your responses should reflect **your own understanding and critical thinking**, not a summarized copied from the paper.
